    About

    IGVA SOLAR ENERGY LTD is a Cypriot company developing large-scale Solar Power Plants. The company is staffed by highly-experienced and specialized team of engineers, electricians, technicians and project managers.

    Staff of the company has successfully developed, designed, constructed and maintained 6 PV Projects in different regions of Ukraine, with a total capacity of 70 MW and already has a portfolio of new projects in Cyprus to be completed in the next years.

    Vision

    As a company, our goal is to produce green energy from renewable sources that will benefit and sustain future generations.

    The ability to secure strategic partnerships with key players in the solar energy business is the basis for the company’s successful development approach.

    The well-maintained collaboration with market key players encourages IGVA to bring together international investors to build mutually fruitful partnerships.

    Mission

    Our mission is to drive the global transition to clean and sustainable energy by developing, solar plants.

    We are committed to delivering exceptional quality, maximizing energy efficiency, and contributing positively to the community's well-being.

    By harnessing the power of the sun, we strive to create a brighter, greener future for generations to come.
